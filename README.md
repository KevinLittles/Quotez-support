## Frequently Asked Questions

### How does Quotez work?
Quotez uses AI to deliver personalized quotes based on your interests and what you want to explore. Simply describe what you're looking for, and we'll find quotes tailored to your needs.

### What's included in the free version?
The free version allows you to view up to 15 quotes per day with full AI personalization.

### What do I get with Quotez Unlimited?
Quotez Unlimited removes all daily limits, giving you unlimited access to AI-personalized quotes whenever inspiration strikes.

### How do I save my favorite quotes?
Tap the heart icon on any quote to add it to your Favorites collection.

### How do I manage my subscription?
Subscriptions are managed through your Apple ID. Go to Settings → [Your Name] → Subscriptions to view or cancel your Quotez Unlimited subscription.

### How do I delete my data?
You can clear your favorites and reading history within the app settings. To completely remove your data, uninstall the app.
